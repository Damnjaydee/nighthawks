Membership by invitation or referral only.

Nighthawks curates intimate dining rooms and after-dark experiences in the most compelling corners of New York City — pairing exceptional settings with chefs, creators, and partners who share our standards of craft and discretion.

Our community is deliberately small and deeply connected. Members include founders, athletes, collectors, engineers, A&Rs, curators, and modern patrons of the night — each selected for character, taste, and contribution to a shared creative ecosystem.

Nighthawks experiences are designed to feel effortlessly rare. Candlelit tables overlooking the skyline, chef-led menus, intelligent pairings, and rooms that move with intention. Beyond dinners, our concierge opens doors — from last-minute reservations and private rooms to cultural access that isn't available to the public.

The Inner Circle hosts rotating dinners and discreet gatherings across the city, with priority access for members and their guests. For those who value privacy and precision, Nighthawks is a trusted counterpart — a society built for people who make the culture, not just attend it.
